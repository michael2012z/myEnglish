# predecessor

A predecessor is something that came before the current version. The person you replaced at work is your predecessor, just like Pac Man is the predecessor of modern video games.
## Definition
- n: one who precedes you in time (as in holding a position or office)
- n: something that precedes and indicates the approach of something or someone
## Family
predecessor: predecessors
## Examples
- Constellation, as it was called, was designed for America’s space goals under Obama’s predecessor, former President George W. Bush.
- Adaptable, consensual, affable, intellectual, Guterres is perhaps better qualified than any of his nine predecessors for the world’s most demanding job.
- Metro’s sleek new stainless steel rail cars are noticeably different from their clanking aluminum predecessors: They’re shiny, brightly lighted, far more modern.

## Family
